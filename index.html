BOTTLING COMPANY

As a bottling company, Starzinger emphasizes high-quality service, reliability, and sustainability. It all started in 1959, when innkeeper Starzinger began bottling beverages, thus laying the foundation for a company marked by solid growth and an eye to the future. Today, it enjoys many years of experience and expertise in this sector. Starzinger Beverage Group is certified as a Leading Nigeria Company.
